The Floor is Lava

This game is a 2D Platformer that I made in a group project with three other students. I was a programmger and level designer for the project. The game's goal is to provide a fun and difficult platforming experience with appealling graphics.

To Greener Pastures

To Greener Pastures is a game I made for Hero Jam 2016 along with four other students. The game is a procedurally-generated, post-apocolyptic survival game. The game takes place in a world in turmoil as one tribe begins to search for a way out of the desert.

Hot Doug

Hot Doug is a 2D Platformer I made with 3 friends during highschool. Hot Doug is the story of a hot dog going on an adventure to save a pair of hot dog buns that he's in love with. (Note: This game predates Seth Rogen's Sausage Fest)
